City Latitude Vs Temperature

This scatter plot graph shows the latitude and max temperature for each city on the date the data was pulled. Using a scatter plot allows for an easy visual and quick analysis on whether a cities latitude(distance from equator) impacts its max temperature.

Analysis: While the temperatures above 40℉ seem to be semi-uniform; only the Northern Hemisphere has max temperatures below 40℉ on the date pulled.
